EZ PARK

Mobile App Design

We aiming to serve UW and matriculated UW students who commute to campus everyday,
or keep their vehicles/ bikes on campus. Moreover, making it easier for these students to
find free and/or affordable parking located around their classrooms. We are investigating
campus accessibility and transportation barriers, as well as the financial burdens that
stem from those barriers. This is a problem because students shouldn’t have to worry about
having to pay an excessive amount for parking.
